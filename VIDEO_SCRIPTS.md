# Week One — Shot-by-Shot Scripts

Six videos. One a day. Each is 20–35 seconds.

**Rules that apply to all of them:**

- Film in daylight, near a window, phone braced on something. Handheld + overhead light is what makes a video read as "low quality" to TikTok.
- **No intro.** The first frame is already the thing. Never "hey guys."
- Say the link out loud once, near the end. Never put it in the caption.
- Film in one take. If you flub it, refilm — don't cut mid-sentence, it hurts watch time.
- If it doesn't feel good while filming, **don't post it.** Spotlight scores your rate, not your volume.

## Day 1 — Multi Balm, bare-face baseline

**This one you can never refilm.** Everything on days 12 and 22 compares back to it. Do it before you use anything.

| Time | Shot | Audio |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:03 | Extreme close-up, bare skin, no makeup, no filter. Turn slowly so light moves across it. | "This is my skin today. No filter, no makeup, nothing on it." |
| 0:03–0:10 | Pull back to full face. Point at the areas you actually want to change. | "Texture here, dark spots here. I've never done anything about either." |
| 0:10–0:18 | Pick up the Multi Balm. Show the packaging clearly for 2 full seconds. | "This is the medicube PDRN balm. It was the number one selling product on TikTok Shop last quarter, which is either a good sign or a marketing thing." |
| 0:18–0:28 | Apply it. Real time, no speeding up. | "So I'm going to use it every day for three weeks and show you exactly what happens. No sponsorship, I bought it." |
| 0:28–0:32 | Back to camera, neutral. | "Come back in three weeks. Link's on my page if you want to start at the same time." |

**Caption:** `day 1 of 21. bare skin, no filter. we'll see.`

> Say "I bought it" only if you did. If a brand ever gifts you something, you must disclose it — FTC rules, and TikTok enforces them.

## Day 4 — Pet fountain, problem → solve

| Time | Shot | Audio |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:04 | Cat walking past a full water bowl, ignoring it. | "She has never once drunk out of this bowl." |
| 0:04–0:10 | You, holding the fountain box. | "Cats are wired to avoid still water. In the wild still water is stagnant water." |
| 0:10–0:20 | Set up the fountain, plug it in, cat approaches. **Wait for it.** | "So you get a cat who's mildly dehydrated for years, and then kidney problems at eight." |
| 0:20–0:30 | Cat drinking. Hold the shot. | "First day. She hasn't left it alone. It's on my page." |

**Caption:** `if your cat ignores their water bowl this is why`

> If the cat doesn't cooperate, don't fake it. Refilm tomorrow. A staged shot reads as fake and tanks watch time.

## After you post

Twenty minutes after each video, pin a comment: `everything's on my page 🔗`

Then leave it alone. Deleting and reposting a slow video resets its distribution and it performs worse the second time.

## End of week one

Open the site, hit **Ctrl+Shift+D**. Whatever has clicks is what week two gets more of. Whatever has zero after two videos comes off the site.
