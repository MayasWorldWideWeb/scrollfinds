# ScrollFinds ‚Äî 30-Day Traffic Plan

The site is done. It converts. It does not create traffic. This file is the part that decides whether this business works.

## The target

```
You earn         $5.65 per sale
$1,000/month  =  177 sales  =  ~177,000 site visits  =  ~5,900/day
```

At a 3‚Äì5% link-click rate from TikTok, **~5,900 site visits/day needs roughly 120,000‚Äì200,000 video views/day.** That is a large account. You have ~1,665 followers.

Month one will not hit $1,000. What month one *can* do is find the 2‚Äì3 products that pull, and get the account posting at a volume where the algorithm can find an audience. Realistic month-one revenue: **$0‚Äì150.** Realistic month-three, if you post daily and one video breaks: **$300‚Äì1,200.**

Use the existing account, not a new one ‚Äî you're above the 1,000-follower TikTok Shop minimum today. A new page is below it.

## Non-negotiables

**Post 2‚Äì3x per day.** Volume is the entire strategy at this stage. Ten good videos beat one perfect one, because you cannot predict which breaks ‚Äî and one break is worth more than the other 200 combined.

**Never say "link in bio" in the caption.** TikTok suppresses reach on posts that push traffic off-platform. Say it *out loud in the video* instead, or put it in a pinned comment 20 minutes after posting.

**Film the product doing the thing.** No talking-head reviews, no slideshows of the product photo. The whole format is: mess ‚Üí tool ‚Üí clean. Problem ‚Üí product ‚Üí solved. Show the payoff in the first 2 seconds, then show how you got there.

**Hook inside 1.5 seconds.** If the first frame is you saying "hey guys," the video is dead. Start mid-action.

Link goes to the site root. Not Linktree ‚Äî every extra tap loses ~40% of clicks.

## Weekly loop (this is the actual system)

Every 7 days:

1. Open the site, press **Ctrl+Shift+D**. Note the top 3 products by clicks.
2. Open TikTok analytics. Note the top 3 videos by *watch time*, not likes.
3. Where those two lists overlap ‚Äî **that's your product.** Make five more videos about it.
4. Anything with zero clicks after two videos, delete from `src/data/products.ts`.

A 12-product site where every product converts beats an 80-product site where nothing does. Cut aggressively.

## When you get approved for affiliate programs

Apply today ‚Äî approvals take days and gate everything:

- **TikTok Shop Creator** ‚Äî in TikTok Studio. You qualify now at 1,665 followers (limited pilot until 5,000). Pays 5‚Äì20%. This is the one that matters.
- **Amazon Associates** ‚Äî instant-ish approval, but you must make 3 sales in 180 days to stay in. Only 1‚Äì4%, so use it just for the four Amazon items.
- **Temu / AliExpress** ‚Äî application review, a few days.

Paste each link into `affiliateUrl` in `src/data/products.ts`. Products without a link are automatically excluded from the live site, so you can deploy today and fill links in as approvals land.
